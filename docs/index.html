About the game

Human forces, leading the Imperial Army, are seeking their spiritual leader, Sigmar Heldenhammer, who was once ago a great soldier of huge power. Last news of him arrived from the far land of Loren, home of the Elves, creatures whose magical powers go beyond imaginable. They are said to live longer than a thousand years, they appear to be so beautiful, so delicated, yet do not attempt to enfuriate them!

Will Humans be able to find their long lost Hero without getting into troubles, or will Elve not welcome them and a new war will start? Find out!

Elf Race

Delicate yet lethal, their speed is legandary and so is their ability with weapons.

Human Race

Sons of Sigmar, their strength has no rival nor has their unstoppable ambition!

Human Stronghold

The maximum representation of glory and splendor, famous for their colleges and army barracks.

Elf Stronghold

Most Elve's magic is found here, were it is taught in combination with fight techinics.

Elf Hero

Their skill is far superior to average Elves. He sees other races as inferiors, no more than mere Orcs and doubts not when it is the time to crush them.

Human Hero

Humans are said to be "sons of Sigmar", but he is the authentic son of Sigmar, he who yields his strength and passion, he who will recover Human's lost Hero.

Play as Elves

The flag color symbolize the high skill they have comunicating with the nature. Choose Elfs and fight for their honor. Leader Elves to avoid Humans taking over your lands, runing your farms and spoiling your ancient magic techinics.

Play as Humans

This is the human flag. The flag color symbolize the strengh and their fight to revenge their ancestors. Choose Humans and fight for their honor. Recover your glory, find your Hero and exterminate!


Two Game Modes

Play all the campaign while progressin along the Human's path or stopping them. Or either go to more direct action by battling over a gigant map!
